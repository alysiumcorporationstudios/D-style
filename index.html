Terms & Purchase

How do I order?

Tap any Order button. You'll be sent to WhatsApp with your item pre-filled. Confirm size, colour, and delivery address with our team.

Payment

Payments are handled directly with the D-Style team over WhatsApp — EFT, cash on delivery (selected areas), or instant bank transfer.

Delivery

Nationwide delivery across South Africa via courier. Delivery fees are quoted based on your area during checkout on WhatsApp.

Returns & exchanges

Unworn items with tags can be exchanged within 7 days of delivery. Sale items are final. Customer covers return shipping.

Sizing

All fits are true-to-size. Reach out on WhatsApp for a full size chart or personal sizing advice.
